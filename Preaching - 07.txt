JESUS loves you.


JESUS loves _you_.

Countless scriptures in the Bible point to this fact. In reality, He died for our sins according to the Scriptures. But GOD demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, CHRIST died for us. In fact He would die _just for you_ because _you_ are that special to Him (see the parable of the lost sheep). You only need to accept Him as your LORD and Saviour to be saved and have eternal life in Heaven when you die.

Because where else are you going to go when you die? Ever thought about that? Do you think you'll just cease to exist? Just blackness and nothing? Or do you believe in some kind of afterlife? But how will you qualify for that, and do you really think it's true?

The only true thing we have is the Word of GOD.
