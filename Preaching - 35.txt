For the Word of GOD is living and active and full of power


The power of reading your Bible is that it is the written Word of GOD, knowing this allows it to prevail in your mind and the HOLY SPIRIT can quicken scriptures to your mind very quickly if you know your Bible well. I liken this to a code book in cryptography acting as a compression mechanism, and GOD through His HOLY SPIRIT can talk to you efficiently this way just giving you book names, chapters and verses which reference a whole lot more text that He wants to communicate with you.

Hebrews 4:12 Amplified Bible:

> **For the word of God is living and active and full of power [making it operative, energizing, and effective]. It is sharper than any two-edged sword, penetrating as far as the division of the soul and spirit [the completeness of a person], and of both joints and marrow [the deepest parts of our nature], exposing and judging the very thoughts and intentions of the heart.**

So read your Bible every day and say the scriptures out loud. Commit them to memory. The sounds of these words come out of your mouth and back into your ears, helping you remember better than if you just read them in your head.
