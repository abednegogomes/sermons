A verse from FATHER GOD


Deuteronomy 4:20 Amplified Bible:

> **But the LORD has taken you and brought you out of the iron [smelting] furnace, out of Egypt, to be a people for His own possession, as [you are] this day.**

If you are a believer, this is you, you are GOD's own possession, His inheritance, His family. I think that is wonderful that FATHER GOD calls us His own sons and daughters through our faith in JESUS CHRIST.
