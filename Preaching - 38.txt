Saved by grace through faith


The principle of GOD's written Word (the Bible) is that it is true. JESUS says Himself in John 14:6 that "I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me." Also in John 1:1-2, we see that: "In the beginning [before all time] was the Word (Christ), and the Word was with God, and the Word was God Himself. He was [continually existing] in the beginning [co-eternally] with God."

These two scriptures are fundamental in understanding why we follow JESUS. In John 3:16 we see: "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." You must believe in JESUS to have eternal life. Hebrews 10:12 states: "But when this priest had offered for all time one sacrifice for sins, he sat down at the right hand of God". If you don't believe in JESUS, you're trying to get to GOD (The FATHER), but have no atonement of your sins. You would be trying to reach heaven via good works (or some other method) which is a false doctrine from the quran and other false religions. Isaiah 64:6 says: "All of us have become like one who is unclean, and all our righteous acts are like filthy rags; we all shrivel up like a leaf, and like the wind our sins sweep us away." Believers are saved by _grace_ through faith, not works, as it says in Ephesians 2:8-9: "For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God — not by works, so that no one can boast."
