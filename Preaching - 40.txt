Call on His name


The bringer of joy is already available to those who call on His name. His name is **JESUS**. And He loves you so much. Just call on Him when you feel down, depressed, discouraged, lost or hopeless. Listen for His voice. Quiet your mind and environment and just listen. Talk with Him. Release your burdens to Him.


Romans 10:13 Amplified Bible:

> **For "WHOEVER CALLS ON THE NAME OF THE LORD [in prayer] WILL BE SAVED."**


Matthew 11:29 Amplified Bible:

> **Take My yoke upon you and learn from Me [following Me as My disciple], for I am gentle and humble in heart, and YOU WILL FIND REST (renewal, blessed quiet) FOR YOUR SOULS.**
